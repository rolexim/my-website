## Professional summary

AWS-certified Cloud Architecture and Infrastructure Engineer with 28+ years of ICT
experience delivering innovative solutions for Fortune 500 companies and cutting-edge
startups. Expertise spans cloud architecture, infrastructure engineering, large-scale
system implementations, and AWS operations. Recently earned the **AWS Certified
CloudOps Engineer – Associate** certification while pursuing additional cloud
credentials.

At **Bankrate (Red Ventures)**, led comprehensive AWS security remediation across 33
accounts and contributed to zero-downtime Terraform module migration affecting 15+
repositories. At **eBay**, independently deployed Goldin Auctions (eBay acquisition)
infrastructure to a new AWS region, resolving critical team blockers and improving
test coverage from 20% to 43%. At **Pinterest**, played a pivotal role in disaster
recovery initiatives, partnering with AWS to build petabyte-scale backup solutions
while contributing to multi-account architecture and IaC automation.

Previously, as Head of Infrastructure at **YPFB Andina**, led a 20+ person team
managing substantial OPEX and CAPEX budgets while pioneering Private Cloud platform
design using OpenStack and Cloud Foundry. Career spans roles at **Crossover**
supporting OSS/BSS solutions for major telecom providers including Telcel Mexico and
Claro Peru, and **Entel** earning recognition for customer-oriented infrastructure
management.
